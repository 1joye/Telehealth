Health Information

Chronic Conditions

Diabetes Mellitus

Clinical Manifestations: Frequent urination, increased thirst, fatigue, blurred vision, slow healing wounds

Prevention & Control: Healthy diet, regular exercise, blood sugar monitoring, medication adherence, regular checkups

Hypertension

Clinical Manifestations: Headaches, shortness of breath, nosebleeds, dizziness, chest pain

Prevention & Control: Reduce salt intake, regular exercise, stress management, regular BP monitoring, medication adherence

Asthma

Clinical Manifestations: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, coughing

Prevention & Control: Avoid triggers, use inhalers as prescribed, monitor symptoms, have an action plan

Pregnancy-related Disorders

Severe Anemia

Clinical Manifestations: Extreme fatigue, weakness, pale skin, shortness of breath, dizziness

Prevention & Control: Iron-rich diet, iron supplements, regular prenatal checkups, blood tests

Gestational Hypertension

Clinical Manifestations: High blood pressure, protein in urine, swelling, headaches, vision changes

Prevention & Control: Regular prenatal care, low-sodium diet, monitoring BP, possible medication, rest

Gestational Diabetes

Clinical Manifestations: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, nausea

Prevention & Control: Healthy diet, regular exercise, blood sugar monitoring, possible insulin

Epidemiological Diseases

Tuberculosis

Clinical Manifestations: Persistent cough, fever, night sweats, weight loss, chest pain

Prevention & Control: BCG vaccination, proper ventilation, complete TB treatment, infection control

Malaria

Clinical Manifestations: High fever, chills, headache, nausea, muscle pain

Prevention & Control: Mosquito nets, insect repellents, antimalarial medication, eliminate breeding sites

COVID-19

Clinical Manifestations: Fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, loss of taste/smell

Prevention & Control: Vaccination, masking, hand hygiene, social distancing, ventilation
